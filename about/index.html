About

Story

Founded by Tammy and Jess in 2024, Second Wave Boutique was born from a shared passion for fashion and a desire to create a unique shopping experience. We wanted to provide a space where teen and young adult women could discover trendy, stylish fashion and accessories without breaking the bank. Our journey began with the belief that great style should be accessible to everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to connect fashion-forward teens and young adults with gently used, name-brand clothing, shoes, and accessories. We aim to promote sustainable fashion by offering high-quality pre-loved items, allowing our customers to expand their wardrobes stylishly and affordably. We are dedicated to fostering a community where fashion is fun, accessible, and environmentally conscious.
